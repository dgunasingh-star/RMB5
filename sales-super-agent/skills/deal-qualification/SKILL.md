# Deal qualification

When a new opportunity appears, run this quick checklist and capture answers in the project's brief.md:

- Budget — is there budget, and how much?
- Authority — who decides, who signs?
- Need — what problem, how urgent?
- Timeline — target decision/close date?

Light MEDDIC extras: Metrics, Economic buyer, Decision criteria, Decision process, Identify pain, Champion.

Flag the single biggest unknown as the next action.
